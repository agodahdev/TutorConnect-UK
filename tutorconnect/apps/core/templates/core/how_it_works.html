How TutorConnect Works

For Students

  1. Search for tutors by subject and location
  2. View profiles, read reviews, and compare rates
  3. Book a lesson at a time that suits you
  4. Pay securely online
  5. Learn and achieve your goals!

For Tutors

  1. Create your profile and verify your identity
  2. Set your subjects, rates, and availability
  3. Receive booking requests from students
  4. Conduct lessons and get paid
  5. Build your reputation with reviews!
